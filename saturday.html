Drop-In Details

The cost is $15 to curl and enjoy snacks that will be provided during intermission.

Open to All: Whether you come with friends or solo, we'll match you up to make a team.

Family-Friendly: A great opportunity for families, couples, and friends to enjoy a non-competitive, entertaining night of curling. Bringing non-members and new curlers is greatly encouraged!

No experience necessary – just bring your enthusiasm!
