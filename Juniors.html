Welcome to Junior Curling 2026/27

All skill levels are welcome!

Junior League (Ages 8–19)

The Lorette Junior Program is a fun community group run by parents and community volunteers.

For our program to run, we will need additional volunteers on the ice this year to help if we want to accept all the families into the program.

If you are able and willing to help on or off the ice, please let us know as soon as possible so we can plan out the program!

For our on-ice volunteers, in the past we brought in a Curl Manitoba instructor for a couple of hours to train the volunteers.

Schedule

We play Saturday mornings from October to March (minimum 16 sessions).

Juniors (Ages 8–19): 10:30–11:30am

Must be 8 years old as of June 30, 2026.

Equipment

We provide all the necessary equipment to get started (brooms, grippers, sliders). Helmets are encouraged.

Curlers and anyone else helping out on the ice are not allowed to wear street shoes on the ice. Please bring non-street shoes — rubber soled shoes are best (running shoes, broomball shoes, etc.).

Registration

Register online through the Lorette Curling Club website.

Existing members: Log into the website and select "Members – Renew Membership" from the menu.

New members: Follow the instructions under "Our Club – New Member Info" on the website.

Print your Registration Receipt and fill out the Assumption of Risk form and bring it to the first session.

All parents or guardians of Junior Program participants must read, agree to, sign, and date an Assumption of Risk form for curling participants under the age of majority.

Visit the Junior Curling page on the club website for full details.
